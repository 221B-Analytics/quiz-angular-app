More About Our
Services

At Oraculi, we believe that it is critical to improve our skills in all aspects of our lives. Our users can quickly connect with experts to get valuable ideas, advice, and answers in a variety of categories. We are passionate about the vision of making knowledge accessible, and our goal is to assist our community in achieving it. We're eager to see how our ideas can help our vision take flight!

Our founder

Oraculi was founded by Sahasra Chinthireddy in 2022. She is a high school student who has extensive knowledge of tutoring services, but she "discovered a gap in being able to find resources available to help elevate my basic life skills." This inspired her to create Oraculi, an online platform that makes such skills easily accessible. Oraculi is Latin for mentor, and the core principle behind our app is the mentor's knowledge that they bring to our platform, as well as how their expertise impacts people and returns positively towards our community.
